About Togdheere

A Trusted Enterprise Driving Growth Across the Horn of Africa

Founded in 2010 and headquartered in Mogadishu, Somalia, Togdheere General Trading & Logistics Co. has grown into one of the region's most respected enterprises. We deliver integrated commercial and development solutions for local and international organizations.

Our diverse portfolio spans general trading, logistics, construction, infrastructure development, procurement, warehousing, freight services, supply chain management, water infrastructure, borehole construction, electronics distribution, and ICT equipment supply.
