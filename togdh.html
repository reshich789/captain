About the Company

Somalia's Most Trusted
Enterprise Partner

Established in 2010 and headquartered in Mogadishu, Togdheere General Trading & Logistics Co. has grown into the Horn of Africa's premier multi-sector enterprise — delivering excellence across logistics, infrastructure, procurement, and humanitarian operations.

📍 Mogadishu HQ 🌍 Horn of Africa 🏆 15+ Years 🌐 UN Verified 📦 500+ Contracts

From international freight forwarding and port clearing to large-scale infrastructure construction, borehole drilling, and humanitarian procurement — our integrated capabilities serve both the private sector and international development agencies. Our deep regional knowledge, experienced professional team, and commitment to ethical business practices make us the partner of choice for organisations working in Somalia and the broader Horn of Africa.
